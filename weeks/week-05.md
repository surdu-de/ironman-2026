## Optional Mobility Work

**Focus:** Hip flexor and hamstring mobility (cycling + running load increasing)
**Duration:** 20-30 min
**Best timing:** Friday evening or Sunday evening after long sessions

**Routine:**
- Hip flexor stretches (couch stretch): 2min each side
- Hamstring flossing: 2x10 each leg
- Pigeon pose: 2min each side
- Cat-cow spine mobility: 2min
- Thoracic rotations: 2x10 each side
- Calf stretching: 2min each leg
- Foam roll IT bands, quads, calves: 5-8min

**Benefits:** Maintains range of motion as training volume increases, particularly targeting areas that tighten from cycling position and running load.

# Z2 Trainer Session 60min

## Description
Indoor trainer session focused on building aerobic base at Zone 2 intensity. Key workout for fat adaptation and steady-state endurance without excessive fatigue.

## Execution
- **Duration:** 60 minutes
- **Heart Rate:** Z2 (140-157 bpm)
- **Power:** 118-156W (60-80% FTP)
- **Cadence:** 85-95 rpm
- **Effort:** Comfortable, can hold conversation

**Structure:**
- 10min: Easy warmup spin, gradually increase to Z2
- 45min: Steady Z2 effort, focus on smooth pedaling
- 5min: Easy cooldown spin

## Success Criteria
- Heart rate stays in Z2 zone (140-157 bpm)
- Power output steady within range (if using power meter)
- Cadence consistent at 85-95 rpm
- Feel fresh and ready for next workout

## Common Variations
- **Shorter:** 45min for recovery weeks
- **Longer:** 75-90min for progression
- **Cadence Work:** Include 5x3min @ 100+ rpm within Z2
- **Erg Mode:** Use smart trainer for consistent power output

## Equipment Setup
- Indoor trainer (wheel-on or direct drive)
- Fan for cooling
- Water bottle
- Heart rate monitor and/or power meter

## Notes
- This is a staple workout throughout the plan
- Focus on maintaining consistent effort, not chasing numbers
- Good opportunity to work on bike fit and position
- Can be done while watching training videos or zone2 content

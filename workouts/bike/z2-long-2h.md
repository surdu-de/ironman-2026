# Z2 Long Ride 2h

## Description
Weekend long ride building aerobic endurance for the Ironman bike leg. Key workout for time in saddle, nutrition practice, and race-specific preparation.

## Execution
- **Duration:** 2 hours
- **Distance:** ~55-60km
- **Heart Rate:** Z2 (140-157 bpm)
- **Power:** 118-156W (60-80% FTP)
- **Cadence:** 85-95 rpm
- **Effort:** Steady, comfortable

**Structure:**
- 10min: Easy warmup spin
- 1h40: Steady Z2 effort, practice race nutrition
- 10min: Easy cooldown

## Success Criteria
- Heart rate stays in Z2 zone throughout
- Power output consistent (if using power meter)
- Successfully implement nutrition strategy
- Feel strong at the end, ready for more

## Common Variations
- **Shorter:** 90min for recovery weeks
- **Longer:** 2h30-3h for peak training phases
- **Terrain:** Include rolling hills in outdoor version
- **Trainer:** Can be split if needed (90min + 30min later)

## Nutrition & Hydration Strategy
**Critical for Ironman prep - use this workout to practice race nutrition!**

- **Carbohydrate Target:** 60g/hour
  - Options: Gels, energy bars, drink mix
  - Start at 30min mark, consume every 20-30min
- **Hydration:** 500-750ml/hour
  - Adjust for temperature and sweat rate
  - Add electrolytes
- **Testing:** Try different products to find what works

**Sample Nutrition Plan:**
- 0:30 - Gel or 1/2 bar (30g carbs)
- 1:00 - Gel or 1/2 bar (30g carbs) + water
- 1:30 - Gel or 1/2 bar (30g carbs)

## Equipment & Setup
- **Indoor:** Trainer, fan, towel, multiple water bottles
- **Outdoor:** Bike, helmet, bottles in cages, nutrition in pockets
- **Tech:** Heart rate monitor, power meter (if available), bike computer

## Notes
- This is THE key workout for Ironman bike preparation
- Mental component is as important as physical
- Practice aero position for extended periods
- Use this to test race day clothing and equipment
- Should finish feeling like you could continue for another hour

# Easy Aerobic Long Run 75min

## Description
Weekend long run focused on building aerobic endurance for the Ironman marathon. Maintains conversational pace throughout while developing mental resilience for long efforts.

## Execution
- **Duration:** 75 minutes
- **Distance:** ~11-12km
- **Heart Rate:** Low Z3 (133-145 bpm)
- **Pace:** 6:15-6:45/km (conversational)
- **Effort:** Comfortable, sustainable

**Structure:**
- Continuous steady effort
- Self-regulated easy pace throughout
- Focus on maintaining form as fatigue sets in

## Success Criteria
- Heart rate stays in Low Z3 zone (133-145 bpm)
- Can maintain conversation throughout
- Form holds up for entire duration
- Recovery within 24-48 hours

## Common Variations
- **Shorter:** 60min for recovery weeks
- **Longer:** 90min for progression phases
- **Terrain:** Include some rolling hills in later cycles
- **Weather:** Adjust pace for heat/humidity, stay in HR zone

## Nutrition & Hydration
- Take water at 30-45min mark
- Optional: Small carb intake after 60min
- Practice race-day nutrition strategies

## Notes
- Key workout for marathon preparation
- Focus on time on feet, not pace
- Last 15min should feel slightly challenging but sustainable
- Recovery run 48 hours later should feel easy
